Capstone Project During 12th Grade

The caption for my group project during our 12th graduation application was an AI-powered notes generator which is a tool that converts educational reading material into detailed and structured notes; for example, lectures, PPTs, and handwritten notes captured using a camera from out mobile phone. It uses AI, OCR, and NLP to extract the key points, elaborate on the concepts, and finally compose the information extracted. Thus making it efficient and accessible on computers and mobile devices, thereby saving students' time, aiding in developing material for educators, and supporting lifelong enabling resources to be more holistic and customized for study by learners. As such, this project supports Sustainable Development Goal 4: Quality Education because it addresses the gaps currently available in the quality of access to learning resources. It helps widen accessibility and address many learning needs, as well as enabling lifelong learning. With features such as multilingual support and real-time transcription, this tool may bring technology closer to helping make education more effective and attractive.
